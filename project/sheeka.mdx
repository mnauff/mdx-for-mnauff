> ## Explanation

Sheeka's Website was made for promoting the PKM league for sheeka

> ## Goals

This project goals is to create landing pages for the Sheeka and Product pages that redirect to the marketplaces.

This project was created using Next.js and TailwindCSS for the frontend. The choice of Next.js was motivated by its built-in SEO capabilities and superior routing compared to plain React. TailwindCSS was employed for its simplicity in handling styling.

> ## The Problems and Lesson Learned

There's no problem that I face. One of the problems is how to deal with image ornaments that difference device may have different positions.
